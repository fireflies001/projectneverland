World Codex

GODDESSES

Shuhua

The goddess of youth. It was believed that she could restore youthfulness to the old.

Minnie

The goddess of love, beauty, justice, and political power. She is the goddess equivalent of Miyeon.

Miyeon

The goddess of beauty, purity, love and fertility. Many believes that lovers who entered her island and passed all her trials will be given a blessing.

Soojin

The goddess of dance, music, joy, sexuality, love and maternal care. Each year people of neverland visit her island to celebrate.

Soyeon

The goddess of duality. Her soft side represented by love, joy, happiness and care. However she also serve as the Guardian of the Mystic, which gave her a vengeful side.

Yuqi

The goddess of Strength, war, justice, and wisdom. she is also the guardian of all living creatures.
